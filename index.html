About the Summit

The Summit on Responsible Decentralized Intelligence brings together leading researchers and innovators to explore how AI/ML and decentralization technologies can collaborate to create more responsible, democratic, and privacy-preserving AI systems.

Hosted by the Berkeley Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI), this multi-disciplinary conference features talks on decentralized ML infrastructure, open source models, personalized AI with privacy, and democratic AI governance.
